Design is my passion. I can make a clear creative solution from a messy idea. Designing creative solutions for customers and deliver a product where I'm proud of. That's what I love to do.

In my third year of the study I went to Denmark to follow a specialization in UX design for six months. This was a great experience. Now I am graduating and I hope to receive my diploma soon.
